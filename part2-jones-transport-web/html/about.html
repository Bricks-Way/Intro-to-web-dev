Our Story

Your professional partner in rubble removal and brick sales

Jones Transport is a dedicated, family-run business deeply rooted in the heart of Cape Town. For over 50 years, we have provided reliable logistics and rubble removal services, building a reputation for honesty, hard work, and efficiency.

What started as a small transport operation has grown into a versatile service provider, supporting the construction industry with high-quality brick supplies and professional site clearing. We treat every project—big or small—with the same care and precision that has defined our family name for generations.

Our Mission & Values

  • Reliability: We show up on time, every time.
  • Quality: Providing only the best Rok Clay and Cement bricks.
  • Community: Proudly serving Summerville and the greater Western Cape area.
